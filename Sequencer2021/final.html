Plan Your Schedule.

To see a recommended class schedule, select your starting term and concentration. This will present a recommended schedule. The first year of your schedule is built in, but the additional terms can be modified to select from program options with your concentration. Note that “important dates” will update based on your term start date to indicate the terms in which you’ll need to complete your program practicum and take the comprehensive exam. If you anticipate needing to take a semester off, please speak with your program advisor regarding options. You can connect with your advisor by selecting “speak to an advisor” in the top right panel. To save your recommended schedule, select “save to PDF.”
